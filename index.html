Our Instructor said that in our class we never think of our classmates as classmates but as a competitor, we never think of our selves as a student but as an entrepeneur.

"ENTREPRENEURSHIP is the process of creating or seizing an opportunity, and pursuing it regardless of the resources currently controlled.""

Source: Entrepreneurship Expert Howard Setevenson

Everyone can be an entrepeneur

Source:https://as2.ftcdn.net/v2/jpg/01/96/87/33/1000_F_196873357_Z8LRgbrFSLloSUP1QEqaVAi3OBbVIaWh.jpg

I learrned that everyone can be an entrepeneur, but not everyone is fit to be one. Not everyone has the same experience as the others, not everyone has the same confidence as the others, being an entrepeneur takes a lot of commitment and determination. People have different skills, ideas, confidence, and mindset. As an entrepeneur, it need not to be selfish even to other competitors but on how a business would benefit the people around you. Ideas change other people's lives.

Generating great Ideas

Source: https://www.crecenegocios.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/que-negocio-poner.jpg

Generating great ideas that would help not for ones benefit but for the community. And skills is what will execute your ideas to reality.

Innovation

Source: https://as2.ftcdn.net/v2/jpg/01/22/04/09/1000_F_122040967_qI53DdWLbtO4o1rAxX12ULOXZcJw78Zn.jpg

making something new to overcome the challenges of change. as time goes by, the demands of people will change to, innovating a product, business or service will help to cope up with the changes of demands of people or they will be introduced to something new.

Coopetition

Source: https://www.saint-gobain.com/en/magazine/stories/companies-work-together-common-good-thats-coopetition

Your idea alone might be okay, but sharing ideas to others will generate more. Collaborating with others, especially with competitors: sharing skills, resources, knowledge, and networks might fill on what the other one is lacking that would lead to a great team.

Trial and Errors

source: https://twitter.com/inspiretheyouth/status/1405837840110567430

Others think that success is a straight line, some might be overwhelmed that if things are running smooth it would lead to a success i. But making a business, you will face ups, downs, and failures. But often times, failures will lead to a great success, you just have to reflect on what went wrong and how to solve it.

"Success is not a goal to reach or a finish line to cross. It is a system to improve, an endless process to refine."

James Clear
